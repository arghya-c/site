About Me

I am a Research Scholar at the School of Technology and Computer Science, Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Mumbai. My advisor is Prof. Prahladh Harsha.

Before joining TIFR, I finished my Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics (BMath) from the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore.

My research interests include Algorithms and their Complexity, mostly Online Algorithms. Graph Matchings with fairness and Online Algorithms with Learning are topics that I am currently working on.
